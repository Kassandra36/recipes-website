Chickpeas with spinach

Catalan chickpeas and spinach is one of my favorite classic tapas here in Barcelona.
It is made with simple ingredients, it is easy to make and it always tastes so comforting and delicious.

Ingredients

To make this recipe you will need:

  • 600g frozen spinach
  • 550g chickpeas
  • 400ml soy or oat milk
  • 1 spoon tapioca flower
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 3 small spoons curry powder
  • 1 small spoon ginger
  • salt & pepper

How to make it

Steps:

  1. Rinse the spinach. Partly to clean the spinach and partly to add a bit of moisture so that the spinach will wilt well once we add it to the sauté.
  2. Sauté the veggies. Cook the onion and garlic in oil until softened, along with the cumin and paprika. Then lower the heat, add in the spinach and cook about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients.Add in those chickpeas and raisins, and then season the whole dish with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve.While the dish is still nice and warm, garnished with pine nuts, a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of paprika.
